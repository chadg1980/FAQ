Weights and Scales

How long does it take to get my scale?
  • The scale should arrive around 10 business days after joining the HealthSlate program.
How do I track my weight with my scale?

Please select the type of scale you have:

BodyTrace scale

Nokia Body (Withings) scale

Aria scale


What are the requirements needed to use my scale?

  • BodyTrace scale
    • There are no requirements to use the BodyTrace scale. Simply stand on the BodyTrace scale and your weight will transmit to the HealthSlate app.

  • Nokia Body (Withings) Body scale
    • The Nokia Body scale must be linked to the Nokia Health Mate app before weight will transmit to the HealthSlate app.
    iOS compatibility: iOS 8 and higher, iPhone 4S and more recent, iPod touch 5th generation and more recent, iPad 3rd generation and more recent.
    Android compatibility: 5.0 and higher.
    Requires Internet access (mobile data or Wi-Fi)

  • Fitbit Aria scale
    • The Aria scale must be linked to a Fitbit app account before weight will transmit to the HealthSlate app.
How do I set a weight goal or target weight?

  1. From the HealthSlate app home screen
    1. iOS device: select More
    2. Android device: select the menu icon in the top left corner of the screen.
  2. Select Settings.
  3. Select Weight Goal and Logging.
  4. Select Target Weight.
  5. Input your desired Target Weight, select Save. (Your Target Weight acts as your weight goal.)
How do I view my weight results?

  1. From the HealthSlate app home screen
    1. iOS device: select More

    2. Select Progress and logs.

    3. Android device: select the menu icon in the top left corner of the screen.
    4. Select Progress and Logs.

  2. Select Weight.
  3. You will now be able to view your weekly weight chart(s).
What does the green and yellow around our picture mean?

  • The colors incidcate how close to your weight loss goal. Green is the progress you have made so far.

How do I delete a weight?

  1. From the HealthSlate home screen, scroll down and select Log Book.
    1. iOS device: select the Weight tab near the top of the screen.

    2. Android device: select Weight from the drop down menu near the top right corner of the screen.
  2. Select the weight you'd like to delete.
  3. Select Delete in the bottom left corner.
